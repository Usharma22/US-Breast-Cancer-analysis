What Is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the breast. It can start in one or both breasts. Cancer starts when cells begin to grow out of control.

How common is breast cancer?

The American Cancer Society's estimates for breast cancer in the United States for 2022 are: About 287,850 new cases of invasive breast cancer will be diagnosed in women. About 43,250 women will die from breast cancer.
